# Quality Control Checklist

Run this checklist before finalizing any response.

## Privacy and Personalization

- [ ] No private candidate information is stored inside the skill itself.
- [ ] No assumptions are made from prior conversations or memory.
- [ ] Candidate evidence comes only from the current invocation.
- [ ] Missing candidate information is identified or handled with stated assumptions.

## Research Integrity

- [ ] Company-specific claims are sourced or clearly marked as inferences.
- [ ] No unsupported generic language such as "prestigious," "global impact," "innovative culture," or "fast-paced environment."
- [ ] Company paragraph contains at least two specific researched details when sources are available.
- [ ] If web access was unavailable, the output clearly states that external company research was not performed.
- [ ] Job description is treated as the source of truth for role-specific needs.

## Candidate Fit Integrity

- [ ] Candidate paragraph contains evidence from the user-provided resume/profile.
- [ ] No fabricated candidate experience, metrics, tools, projects, responsibilities, or credentials.
- [ ] No overstatement of weak or transferable qualifications.
- [ ] Resume evidence is interpreted, not merely repeated.
- [ ] Weak, missing, or non-obvious match areas are handled honestly in Bridge and Expansion Ideas.

## Cover Letter Quality

- [ ] The letter follows the four-paragraph structure.
- [ ] The tone fits U.S. recruiting norms.
- [ ] The letter is specific, confident, and professional.
- [ ] The letter is approximately 350-450 words unless otherwise requested.
- [ ] The final output includes Research Brief, Match Matrix, Final Cover Letter, and Customization Notes.
- [ ] Bridge and Expansion Ideas appears only when useful.
