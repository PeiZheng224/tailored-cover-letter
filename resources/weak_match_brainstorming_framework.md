# Weak-Match Brainstorming Framework

Use this framework when the role has weak, missing, or non-obvious match areas.

## Purpose

Help the user think honestly about transferable evidence. Do not pretend the candidate has experience they did not provide.

## Bridge and Expansion Ideas Format

```markdown
## C. Bridge and Expansion Ideas

| Weaker area | Honest bridge | Additional evidence to look for | Candidate can confirm or replace |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Gap] | [Transferable framing] | [Possible evidence category] | [Speculative idea or verification prompt] |
```

## Brainstorming Sources

Suggest possible evidence from:

- School
- Internships
- Projects
- Entrepreneurship
- Research
- Volunteering
- Leadership
- Prior work
- Client, customer, or stakeholder interactions
- Data analysis, writing, presentation, or operations experience

## Rules

- Identify the gap directly.
- Frame transferable experience without exaggeration.
- Suggest what additional evidence the candidate could provide if available.
- Mark speculative ideas as "candidate can confirm or replace."
- Do not fabricate experience.
- Do not overstate qualifications.
- If the gap is important enough to affect the letter, avoid centering that area in the final draft unless the user provides stronger evidence.
